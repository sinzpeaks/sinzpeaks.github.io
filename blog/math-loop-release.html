Math Loop: A Fresh Take on Logic & Numbers

Date: November 28, 2025

Author: SINz Peaks

What is Math Loop?

Math Loop is our upcoming logic-based puzzle game designed to sharpen your mind through simple but engaging number loops. It's built with a minimal neon visual style and smooth transitions to give players a calming, brain-boosting experience.

Why We're Building It
