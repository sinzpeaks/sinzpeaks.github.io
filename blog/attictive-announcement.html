Introducing Attictive: Our Next Arcade Masterpiece

Date: February 9, 2026

Author: SINz Peaks

What is Attictive?

After the success of Math Loop, we're diving into a completely different experience: Attictive. This upcoming arcade-style game brings fast-paced, skill-based gameplay that challenges you to beat your own records and compete with friends worldwide.

Core Features

  • Live leaderboards to track your ranking globally
  • Challenge friends and compete for supremacy
  • Limited lives system that keeps every moment intense
  • Skill-based gameplay—no luck, pure performance
  • Adaptive difficulty that scales to your ability
  • Quick play sessions perfect for a break or marathon gaming

Our Vision

At SINz Peaks, we believe arcade games should be about pure gameplay and genuine skill. Attictive is crafted with passion, polish, and the core belief that great games don't need gimmicks—they need to be:

"Whether you're playing for a quick break or chasing a high score, the game adapts to your skill and keeps you engaged. It's not about luck. We're crafting this game with passion, polish, and pure gameplay at its core."

What to Expect

Attictive will combine the minimalist aesthetic we perfected with Math Loop with high-intensity arcade mechanics. Expect smooth animations, responsive controls, and a gameplay loop that keeps you coming back for "just one more round."

Current Development Status

We're actively developing Attictive and putting extensive work into level design, game balance, and ensuring the mechanics are as tight as possible. Our goal is to deliver an arcade experience that's both accessible to new players and challenging for veterans.
