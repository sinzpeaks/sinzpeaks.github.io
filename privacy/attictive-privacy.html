SINz Peaks ("we", "our", or "us") built Attictive as a free-to-play mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects user information.

By downloading or playing Attictive, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as your real name, email address, or phone number directly through the app.

However, the game uses third-party services that may collect certain data automatically to provide core functionality and advertising.

1.1 Data Collected Through PlayFab

Attictive uses PlayFab (Microsoft) to provide online features such as leaderboards.

  • Player display name
  • Unique player ID
  • Gameplay data (scores, rankings)
  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • IP address

This data is used to provide game functionality, maintain leaderboards, improve performance, and prevent cheating or abuse.

1.2 Data Collected Through Google AdMob (Future Use)

Attictive may use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data and ad interactions
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Approximate location (non-precise)

This data is collected by Google for delivering ads, measuring ad performance, and preventing fraud. We do not directly control this data.

For more information, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Information

Information collected may be used for:

  • Providing online leaderboard functionality
  • Saving gameplay progress
  • Improving game stability and performance
  • Displaying and measuring advertisements
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

We do not sell or trade personal information.

3. Children's Privacy

Attictive is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

4. Third-Party Services

The game may use the following third-party services:

  • PlayFab (Microsoft)

Each third-party service processes data according to its own privacy policies.

We rely on trusted third-party providers that implement industry-standard security measures. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.
