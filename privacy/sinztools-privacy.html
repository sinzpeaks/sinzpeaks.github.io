Introduction

Welcome to SinzTools ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection

SinzTools is designed to work entirely offline on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users. All image processing, PDF operations, QR code generation, and other tool functions are performed locally on your device.

Any files you process using SinzTools (images, PDFs, videos, etc.) are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to these files, and they are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

SinzTools may request the following permissions to function properly:

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the app and no data is collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

SinzTools does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of the app is completely private.
